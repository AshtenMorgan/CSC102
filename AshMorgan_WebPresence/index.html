ASHTEN

Origin: Louisiana, United States, Earth






Hey! Let me introduce myself...

My name is Ashten Morgan (yes, with an E), and I hail from the cotton capital of the United States,
Louisiana. Growing up, I was always known for drawing and painting and for my love of video games, so
that was foundational as I grew in my hobbies and started thinking about my future. Despite the many
inevitable challenges and uncertainties, I was always certain that I wanted to do cool things with art.

For as long as I can remember, I've been drawing characters and icons from my favorite games and
media, ranging from Samus Aran of Metroid (a personal favorite) to my iconic "custom colossi designs"
inspired by the monolithic beings from the Shadow of the Colossus game. I remember when I was
around the age of 13, my stepmom asked me and my siblings what we wanted to be when we grew up.
My older brother said he wanted to be a professional wrestler. My younger sisters wanted to be
princesses and explorers.

I wanted to be, as I put it, a "game designer".

At the age of 18, I began pursuing a BA in Game Art & Animation, and this site is dedicated to my best
work.

Welcome to my portfolio.
